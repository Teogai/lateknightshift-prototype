# CARDS

- See `GAME_DESIGN.md` for card rules
- Format per card: `- <name> | cost <n> | type <move/summon/signature> | effect: <one-liner>`

## Implemented

- Move | cost 1 | type move | effect: move any friendly piece to a legal square
- Move | cost 0 | type move | effect: free move (combo support)
- Summon Pawn | cost 1 | type summon | effect: place pawn on ranks 1–2
- Summon Knight | cost 2 | type summon | effect: place knight on rank 1
- Summon Bishop | cost 2 | type summon | effect: place bishop on rank 1
- Summon Rook | cost 3 | type summon | effect: place rook on rank 1

## Starter decks (10 cards each)

### The Knight
- 5x Move(1), 2x Move(0), 1x Summon Knight(2), 1x Summon Pawn(1), 1x Summon Rook(3)

### The Bishop
- 5x Move(1), 2x Move(0), 1x Summon Bishop(2), 2x Summon Pawn(1)
